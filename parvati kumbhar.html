My Story

My name is Parvati Kumbhar, and I am a student of Class 10. I enjoy learning new things and spending my free time doing activities that make me happy. One of my favourite hobbies is cooking. I love helping my family in the kitchen and trying new recipes. Cooking teaches me patience, creativity, and teamwork.

Another hobby that I enjoy is playing Kabaddi. It keeps me active, healthy, and strong. Whenever I play with my friends, I learn the importance of discipline, confidence, and sportsmanship. Kabaddi has helped me become more energetic and determined.

I also love travelling. Exploring new places allows me to learn about different cultures, foods, and traditions. Every journey gives me new experiences and beautiful memories. Whether it is a short trip with my family or visiting a nearby village, travelling always excites me.

These hobbies make my life enjoyable and meaningful. They help me learn valuable lessons, stay active, and discover the world around me. I hope to continue improving my skills, exploring new destinations, and creating wonderful memories in the future.
